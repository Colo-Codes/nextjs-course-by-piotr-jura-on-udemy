# Favourite Dog Breeds

Dogs come in all shapes, sizes, and personalities, making them one of the most diverse species on the planet. From loyal companions to energetic playmates, there's a breed for every lifestyle. Here are some of the most popular dog breeds that have captured the hearts of dog lovers worldwide.

## Labrador Retriever

![Labrador Retriever](https://unsplash.com/photos/adult-yellow-labrador-retriever-c7bUIRBqapA)

**Temperament:** Friendly, outgoing, and eager to please.

**Size:** Medium to large.

**Lifespan:** 10-12 years.

**Highlights:** Labs are known for their gentle nature, intelligence, and versatility. They excel as family pets, service dogs, and working dogs in various fields.

## German Shepherd

![German Shepherd](https://unsplash.com/photos/a-close-up-of-a-dog-with-its-tongue-out-BtwFA_v1xg4)

**Temperament:** Intelligent, loyal, and protective.

**Size:** Large.

**Lifespan:** 9-13 years.

**Highlights:** German Shepherds are prized for their courage, loyalty, and versatility. They are often used in police and military work, search and rescue operations, and as service dogs.
